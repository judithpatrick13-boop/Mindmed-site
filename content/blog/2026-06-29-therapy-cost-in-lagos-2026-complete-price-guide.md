Thinking about therapy in Lagos but worried about cost? You’re not alone. In 2026, more Lagosians are choosing mental health support, but “how much does therapy actually cost?” is still the #1 question.

This guide breaks down real prices in Lagos for 2026. We’ll cover private therapist rates, online vs in-person costs, if **NHIS/NHIA** covers sessions, and 3 ways to make therapy affordable even on a budget. No fluff, just numbers you can use today.

##  Average Therapy Cost in Lagos 2026

### Private Therapist Per Session:

\- **Online therapy:** **₦8,000 - ₦15,000** per **50-min** session

\- **In-person Lagos: ₦12,000 - ₦25,000** per session 

\- **Top-tier/clinic: ₦30,000 - ₦50,000** per session

Why the difference? Location in Lagos, therapist experience, and whether it’s a clinic vs private practice.

## Online vs In-Person: Which Costs Less?

Online therapy is **20-40%** cheaper because therapists don’t pay office rent.

**Online Pros:** Lower cost, no Lagos traffic, evening sessions available  

**In-Person Pros:** Better for severe cases, no internet/data needed

## Does NHIS/NHIA Cover Therapy in Nigeria 2026?

Short answer: Basic NHIS/NHIA covers some mental health, but coverage is limited.

**Covered:** Psychiatrist consultation, some medications  

**Not Covered:** Most talk therapy/counselling sessions with psychologists

Most Nigerians pay out-of-pocket for therapy. Some HMOs now offer mental health add-ons - check with yours.

##   3 Ways To Make Therapy Affordable

1. **Sliding Scale:** Many Lagos therapists reduce fees if you earn less. Just ask during booking.
2. **Student/NGO Programs:** Universities + NGOs like Mentally Aware offer free/low-cost sessions
3. **Payment Plans:** Platforms like MindMed let you pay per session instead of buying packages

## Is Therapy Worth The Cost?

Think of it like gym for your mind. 1 session/week at **₦12,000 = ₦48,000**/month max. 

Compare that to cost of burnout, anxiety, or stress affecting work/relationships.

Many clients report that 6-8 sessions create changes that last years.

##  FAQ

##### Q1: How much is therapy per month in Lagos?

If you go weekly at **₦12,000/session**, budget **₦48,000/month**. Biweekly = **₦24,000/month.**

##### Q2: Is therapy covered by insurance in Nigeria?

Most HMOs don’t cover talk therapy yet in 2026. NHIS covers psychiatry/medication only. Always confirm with your HMO.

##### Q3: Can I get free therapy in Lagos?

Yes. Check university counselling centers, NGOs like Mentally Aware Nigeria.
