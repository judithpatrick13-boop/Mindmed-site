## NDPR: The Nigerian Law Protecting Your Therapy Data

**NDPR = Nigeria Data Protection Regulation**. It’s been law since 2019, and it treats mental health data as “sensitive”.

**What that means for you:**

1. **We ask first** :You must consent before we collect anything. That’s why you’ll see a form before the MindMed test.
2. **We use it for one reason only:** To match you with a therapist + support your care. Not ads. Not analytics.
3. **You’re in control:** Want your data deleted? You can ask. We must comply by law.

If a website or app doesn’t mention NDPR or consent, that’s your first red flag.
