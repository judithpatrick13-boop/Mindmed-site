Privacy Policy & NDPR Compliance

Last Updated: April 25, 2026

1. NDPR Compliance

MindMed complies with the Nigeria Data Protection Regulation NDPR 2019. Your data is processed lawfully, fairly, and transparently. We are registered data controllers.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly through our website forms: name, email, phone, and mental health screening responses. This data is stored securely in our own encrypted database and never sold to third parties.

3. Purpose of Data

Your data is used to: provide screening results, contact you about therapy booking, and improve services. We never sell or share your data with third parties for marketing.

4. Your Rights Under NDPR

You have the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of your data. Email support@mindmed.com.ng to exercise these rights.

5. Medical Disclaimer

MindMed provides educational content and screening tools only. This is not medical advice or diagnosis. For emergencies, call 112.
